Comprehensive Support Services

At C&J Provider Services, we provide a range of support services designed to meet the unique needs of individuals with developmental disabilities. Our person-centered approach ensures that each individual receives the specific supports they need to thrive and achieve their personal goals.

Our team of dedicated professionals work collaboratively with individuals, families, and community partners to deliver high-quality services that promote independence, foster growth, and enhance quality of life.

Adult Day Services

Our Adult Day Services provide a structured, supportive environment where individuals can participate in meaningful activities, develop life skills, and build social connections. We offer program locations in Mentor, OH and Beverly, OH (as "It's Your Choice").

What We Offer:

  • Personalized activity planning based on individual interests and goals
  • Life skills development including cooking, budgeting, and personal care
  • Community integration activities and outings
  • Creative expression through art, music, and other mediums
  • Recreational activities and exercise programs
  • Social skills development and peer interaction

Our day programs operate Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, providing a consistent routine and reliable support for participants and their families.

Transportation Services

We provide reliable, safe, and accessible transportation services to ensure individuals can access community resources, attend appointments, and participate in recreational activities.

What We Offer:

  • Wheelchair accessible vehicles equipped with modern safety features
  • Trained, compassionate drivers who understand special needs
  • Door-to-door service for maximum convenience
  • Transportation to medical appointments, day programs, and community activities
  • Flexible scheduling to accommodate individual needs
  • Regular vehicle maintenance and safety checks

Our transportation services are designed to eliminate barriers to community access and ensure that individuals can participate fully in their communities.

Residential Services

Our residential services provide individuals with a supportive home environment that promotes independence, personal choice, and community integration while ensuring safety and well-being. We operate residential homes in Mentor, OH and surrounding areas.

What We Offer:

  • 24/7 supportive care from trained staff
  • Home-like environments that foster independence
  • Person-centered planning for daily activities and goals
  • Independence skill building in areas such as cooking, cleaning, and personal care
  • Community integration and recreational opportunities
  • Support for maintaining relationships with family and friends

Our residential services are designed to provide a balance of support and independence, allowing individuals to live as autonomously as possible while receiving the assistance they need.

Inquire About Residential Services

Homemaker Personal Care (HPC)

Our Homemaker Personal Care services provide individualized support to help people with developmental disabilities live more independently in their own homes or family homes.

What We Offer:

  • Assistance with activities of daily living (ADLs)
  • Support with household management and maintenance
  • Meal planning, preparation, and grocery shopping support
  • Medication reminders and health monitoring
  • Community access and participation support
  • Skills training to increase independence

Our HPC services are customized to meet each individual's specific needs and preferences, providing just the right level of support to maximize independence and quality of life.
