About C&J Provider Services

Creating opportunities and enhancing lives since 2021

Who We Are

C&J Provider Services is a leading provider of comprehensive support services for individuals with developmental disabilities. We are committed to promoting independence, fostering personal growth, and enhancing the quality of life for each person we serve.

Our Mission

We promote self-awareness by allowing our individuals freedom to explore all the possibilities that life offers. We pride ourselves on developing a daily schedule around the desires and needs of the people we serve.

Our Services

Comprehensive support tailored to individual needs

Adult Day Services

Our Adult Day Services provide a safe, engaging, and supportive environment where individuals can participate in meaningful activities, develop life skills, and build social connections.

  • Personalized activity planning
  • Life skills development
  • Community integration
  • Creative expression programs
  • Wheelchair accessible vehicles
  • Trained, compassionate drivers
  • Door-to-door service
  • Flexible scheduling

Day Habilitation

Structured programs designed to provide opportunities for individuals to develop and maintain skills needed for community integration.

Learn More
  • 24/7 supportive care
  • Home-like environments
  • Person-centered planning
  • Independence skill building

Residential Services

Our residential services provide individuals with a supportive home environment that promotes independence, personal choice, and community integration while ensuring safety and well-being.

  • 24/7 supportive care
  • Home-like environments
  • Person-centered planning
  • Independence skill building
