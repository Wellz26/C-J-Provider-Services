Who We Are

Founded in 2021, C&J Provider Services has quickly established itself as a leading provider of comprehensive support services for individuals with developmental disabilities in Ohio.

Our team consists of dedicated professionals who are passionate about making a positive difference in the lives of the individuals we serve. We bring together expertise in healthcare, social services, and community integration to deliver holistic care that addresses the unique needs of each person.

At C&J Provider Services, we believe that every individual deserves to live a fulfilling life with dignity, respect, and opportunities for growth. This belief guides everything we do, from our personalized care plans to our community engagement activities.

Our Mission

We promote self-awareness by allowing our individuals freedom to explore all the possibilities that life offers. We pride ourselves on developing a daily schedule around the desires and needs of the people we serve.

Our mission is to provide exceptional support services that enhance the quality of life for individuals with developmental disabilities, while promoting independence, dignity, and community integration.

Our Vision

To be the premier provider of comprehensive support services for individuals with developmental disabilities, recognized for our person-centered approach, innovative programs, and commitment to fostering independence and community engagement.

We envision a world where all individuals with developmental disabilities have access to the supports they need to live fulfilling lives as valued members of their communities.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our approach to providing exceptional care

Compassion

We approach our work with genuine care and empathy, recognizing the unique needs and challenges of each individual we serve.

Dignity & Respect

We honor the inherent worth and dignity of each person, treating them with respect and supporting their right to make choices about their own lives.

Community

We believe in the importance of meaningful community connections and work to facilitate integration and participation in community life.

Excellence

We are committed to providing the highest quality services, continuously improving our programs, and exceeding the expectations of those we serve.
